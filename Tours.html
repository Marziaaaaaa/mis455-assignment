Whitewater Raftingin the Amazon

This three-day excursion combines jungle hikes with a world class Amazon river rafting adventure that runs through two of Ecuador’s very best one-day river runs. Located on the eastern flanks of the Andes Mountains, this region is perfect for white water rafting, as the landscape slowly meanders down from the high mountains to the Amazon River Basin. The Jatunyacu is a classic descent through breathtaking jungle scenery with Kichwa Indian families settled along its shores. This descent is followed by a combined stretch of the Jondachi and Hollin rivers which offer exciting rapids
